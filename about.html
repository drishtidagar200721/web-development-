About Us

Pahredar Foundation is a nationally recognized non-profit organization dedicated to protecting civil and legal rights while promoting education, environmental conservation, and social welfare. We believe every individual deserves equal opportunities, justice, and a safe environment.

Our Vision

To build a society where every person is aware of their rights, empowered through knowledge, and inspired to contribute to a better future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create positive social change through legal awareness, education, environmental initiatives, community development, and volunteer-driven programs.

Our Values

  • Integrity and Transparency
  • Equality and Justice
  • Community Service
  • Environmental Responsibility
  • Empowerment through Education

Why Choose Pahredar Foundation?

We work closely with communities to spread awareness, provide support, encourage volunteerism, and create sustainable solutions that improve lives.
